Read ABOUT ME

My name is Nekesa Martha, a third-year student at the African Leadership University, Mauritius, majoring in Computing Science. I am currently interning remotely with Nadine Meisel. Nadine Meisel is a strategic design and innovation consulting company located in Spain.

The company uses elements of design thinking through Lateral Shortcuts, which is a technologically innovative, effective and easy solution to provide a human-centered approach to clients.

I was assigned to work with the Marketing team, whereby I focused on analyzing the data collected through the company’s social media channels, the Key Performance Indicators and also the sales made. After analyzing, I would make databases and guesstimations on the expected sales. During the internship period, I got to learn how to use data tools such as Google Data Studio which is now a game changer in the world of business intelligence.

Skills

To get the internship with Nadine Meisel, the company was looking for individuals with a combination of marketing, data analysis and research skills set.
