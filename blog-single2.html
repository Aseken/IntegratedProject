We don't get what we want, we get what we have to have...-2



Been a minute since we were last here.I have been busy adulting, some little breaks here and there have been necessary. Turns out my internship will be a remote one. After working tirelessly on the market analysis pitch hoping to score a flight to Barcelona even for a month, all I got was sorry we can only have you as a remote intern since we are not in a position to fly you currently.

So all the places in my bucket list I was looking forward to visiting will remain where they are for now and that is in my bucket list. Anyway, in the meantime, I can get a day job since the remote internship is not demanding at all. Visit friends, tour the island and feel the warm onesie and enjoy the tranquility, or just get something going for me as I wait for these remaining month to end.
